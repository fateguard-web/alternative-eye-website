## Charles Ray Vines Identified as Prime Suspect in Multiple Decoded Chapter 8 Cipher Victims

New forensic analysis has identified **Charles Ray Vines**, also known as the **River Valley Killer**, as a **prime suspect in three decoded “Chapter 8” cipher victims**, with compelling indicators of a possible operational and communicative connection to **Dennis Rader (BTK)**.

### Dennis Rader and Charles Vines Side by Side

![Dennis Rader and Charles Vines Side by Side](/assets/dennis-rader-charles-vines-side-by-side.jpg)

The investigation is being led by forensic profiler and investigator Melissa Phelan, host of Alternative Eye, alongside her investigative team, utilizing a novel decoding methodology combined with scientific replication techniques to strengthen forensic relevance.

### Charles Ray Vines

![Dennis Rader and Charles Vines Side by Side](/assets/charles-ray-vines.png)

One of the newly decoded Chapter 8 victims includes **Melissa Witt**, who was abducted from **Bowling World in Fort Smith, Arkansas, in 1994**, and later found murdered. Charles Ray Vines was a **known prime suspect** in Witt’s case, as well as in two additional victims now linked through the decoded cipher material. **Melissa Witt has also been matched to known sketches of Dennis Rader- depicting a woman bound and gagged with the tag line “THE SEXUAL THRILL IS MY BILL”.**
